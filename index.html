Our Mission

JSAFE Canada is a federally incorporated not-for-profit organization dedicated to the safety, empowerment, and dignity of Jewish Canadians. Through community-based self-defense and security, legal firearms education, and principled advocacy, we provide the tools and training our communities need to remain strong, confident, and protected.
