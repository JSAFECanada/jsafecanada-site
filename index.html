Our Mission

JSAFE Canada will soon be a federally incorporated not-for-profit organization dedicated to the safety, empowerment, and dignity of Jewish Canadians. Through community-based self-defense and security, legal firearms education, and principled advocacy, we provide the tools and training our communities need to remain strong, confident, and protected.

Our Focus Areas

Community physical wellness and accessible self-defense programs for safety and preparedness.

Educational training in legal firearm use and safety through licensed ranges and certified instructors.

Advocating for Jewish Canadians of all ages to feel secure, supported, and empowered in daily life.
